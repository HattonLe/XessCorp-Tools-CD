The XS40-005E+ Board is perfect for experimenting with FPGA designs, microcontroller programming, or hardware/software codesign.  The 9,000-gate XC4005E FPGA operates at 5V so you can connect it to commonly available TTL chips.  Digital logic designs can be loaded into the FPGA.  The microcontroller can use the FPGA as a coprocessor.  The 128-KByte SRAM can store microcontroller programs/data or serve as general-purpose storage for FPGA-based designs.

The XC4005E series of FPGAs is supported by Xilinx's Foundation and Alliance Series.
