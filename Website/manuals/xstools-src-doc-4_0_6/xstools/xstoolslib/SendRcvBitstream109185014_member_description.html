Description
Transmit a bitstream through TDI while receiving a bitstream through TDO.

This subroutine assumes the TAP controller state is Shift-IR or Shift-DR upon entry. Upon termination, this subroutine will leave the TAP controller in the Exit1-IR or Exit1-DR state.

Either sendBits or rcvBits can be a zero-length bitstream if you don't want to transmit or receive bits during a particular call to this subroutine. For example, you may want to load the BSDR with a bit pattern but you might not care what data gets shifted out of the BSDR during the loading.

SendBits and RcvBits can point to the same bitstream without causing problems.

The LSB of a bitstream has an index of 0.
