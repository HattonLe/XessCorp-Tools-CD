Errors caused by incorrect bus format in the UCF constraints

Q:

I am using foundation 1.5 on win95. When I implement LEDDCD40, I get the constraints error:

ERROR:bascp:87 - Could not find NET "D0" in design "cleanup1".  NET entry is
"NET D0      LOC=P44;

This error continues along the entire list of D's and S's

These are the files I am using

NET D0  LOC=P44;
NET D1  LOC=P45;
NET D2  LOC=P46;
NET D3  LOC=P47;
NET S0  LOC=P25;
NET S1  LOC=P26;
NET S2  LOC=P24;
NET S3  LOC=P20;
NET S4  LOC=P23;
NET S5  LOC=P18;
NET S6  LOC=P19;

library IEEE;
use IEEE.std_logic_1164.all;

entity leds is
port(
	D: in  STD_LOGIC_VECTOR (3 downto 0);
	S: out STD_LOGIC_VECTOR (6 downto 0)
);
end leds;

architecture leds_arch of leds is

begin
	with D select
	S <= "1110111" when "0000",   --0
		"0010010" when "0001",   --1
		"1011101" when "0010",   --2
		"1011011" when "0011",   --3
		"0111010" when "0100",   --4
		"1101011" when "0101",   --5
		"1101111" when "0110",   --6
		"1010010" when "0111",   --7
		"1111111" when "1000",   --8
		"1111011" when "1001",   --9
		"0000000" when others; --blank display
end leds_arch;

Do you know a solution to this problem?

A:

Yes, your constraints are using the wrong format. Try the following format:

NET D<0>  LOC=P44;
NET D<1>  LOC=P45;
NET D<2>  LOC=P46;
NET D<3>  LOC=P47;
NET S<0>  LOC=P25;
NET S<1>  LOC=P26;
NET S<2>  LOC=P24;
NET S<3>  LOC=P20;
NET S<4>  LOC=P23;
NET S<5>  LOC=P18;
NET S<6>  LOC=P19;
